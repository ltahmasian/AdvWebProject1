The 5-Step Reset — Pick a Step

Clear the surface.
Remove everything that doesn’t belong. If you haven’t touched it today, put it away. Start with a blank slate.

Manage your cables.
Bundle and route cables behind the desk. One loose cable makes the whole setup look messy.

Place only your essentials.
Put back only what you actively use: laptop, notebook, one pen. Everything else earns its spot later.
